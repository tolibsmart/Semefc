Our History

Founded: 2021

Location: Seme, Katani, Machakos County, Kenya

Seme Football Sporting FC was founded in 2021 with the vision of nurturing grassroots football talent in Seme. Our mission is to provide a platform where young athletes can develop their skills, build discipline, teamwork, and represent the community with pride.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To nurture grassroots football talent and promote discipline, teamwork, and community values.

Vision: To become a leading community-based football club producing top players for Kenya and beyond.
